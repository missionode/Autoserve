Help and FAQ

Quick guidance for every step of your order.

Browse and order

Choose an available menu item, select its size and options, then review your order.

Dine-in or takeaway

Dine-in requires a valid table reference. Takeaway orders use the customer name and mobile number.

Payment and token

A token is allocated only after simulated UPI success. Failed, cancelled, or pending attempts do not deduct stock.

Tracking

Follow Payment confirmed → Received → Preparing → Ready → Delivered. Keep your token available at collection.

Tic-Tac-Toe rewards

Signed-in customers receive one eligible attempt per paid active order. Guests can play for practice only.

Unavailable items

Sold-out and emergency-cutoff items cannot be ordered. Your order is checked again before payment.

Recovery: If the restaurant is closed or data cannot be loaded, your order is retained so you can return later or sign in again. Pending payments must be resolved before an order is created.
